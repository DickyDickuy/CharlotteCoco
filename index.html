About Us

Charlotte Essence is a brand of PT Roxy Castello Group, and a trusted Indonesian exporter specializing in premium coconut sugar and natural sweeteners. Sourced directly from local farmers in Indonesia's finest coconut and sugar palm regions, our products are harvested using traditional methods and processed with minimal intervention to preserve nutrients, authentic taste, and natural caramel flavor.

Committed to sustainability and food safety, we adhere to HACCP standards and are certified Halal Indonesia, and ISO 22000:2018.

Serving diverse markets from retailers to hospitality and wholesale buyers across Asia, Europe, and the Americas, Charlotte Essence delivers trusted, traceable, and environmentally conscious coconut-based products to meet the growing global demand for healthier sweeteners.
