About Us

Charlotte Essence is a brand of PT Roxy Castello Group, and a trusted Indonesian exporter specializing in premium coconut sugar and natural sweeteners. Sourced directly from local farmers in Indonesia's finest coconut and sugar palm regions, our products are harvested using traditional methods and processed with minimal intervention to preserve nutrients, authentic taste, and natural caramel flavor.

Committed to sustainability and food safety, we adhere to HACCP standards and are certified Halal Indonesia, and ISO 22000:2018.

Serving diverse markets from retailers to hospitality and wholesale buyers across Asia, Europe, and the Americas, Charlotte Essence delivers trusted, traceable, and environmentally conscious coconut-based products to meet the growing global demand for healthier sweeteners.

Premium Palm Sugar

Palm Sugar is a natural sweetener derived from the sap of the Arenga palm tree, offering a rich caramel-like flavor, natural brown color, and authentic aroma that distinguish it from coconut sugar and cane sugar. It is available in powder form (granulated sugar) ideal for beverages and bakery, liquid form for drinks, and molded blocks or cubes in coin, bamboo, or bowl shapes commonly used in both traditional and modern cuisine. Premium Coconut Sugar

Coconut Sugar is a natural sweetener made from the nectar of coconut blossoms through a traditional yet hygienic process, resulting in golden-brown crystals with a mild caramel flavor. With a lower Glycemic Index than refined sugar and natural minerals such as potassium, zinc, and iron, coconut sugar offers a healthier and sustainable alternative to white sugar. Perfect for beverages, bakery, desserts, and modern cuisines, Charlotte Essence Coconut Sugar brings Indonesia's natural sweetness to the world.

Liquid Palm Sugar

Liquid Palm Sugar is a natural liquid sweetener made from the sap of palm trees, processed through a traditional yet hygienic method to produce a thick, golden-brown syrup with a distinctive caramel flavor. Rich in natural minerals such as potassium, calcium, and iron, this palm sugar syrup serves as a healthier alternative to refined syrups. With its authentic sweetness, Liquid Palm Sugar is ideal for beverages like coffee, tea, and smoothies, as well as a wide range of culinary applications from desserts and sauces to both traditional and modern cuisines.
